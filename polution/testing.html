Welcome to ANAND ENVIRONMENTAL CONSULTANTS PVT. LTD.

Active since 1978 in India, Afghanistan, Bahrain, Bangladesh, Bhutan and Uganda. An ISO 9001:2008, ISO/IEC: 17205:2005, Quality Council of India (NABET) certified company. Provided services to the Royal Government of Bhutan. MOU with Ugandan company to provide services in Uganda. Guided by US educated and experienced management. Technical staff of over 20 people. In-house laboratory facilities for treatability studies. Pollution control facilities, equipment and services provided to over 500 industries. Pollution control facilities even for 1,00,00,000 litres per day wastewater or as low as 1000 litres per day. Technology adopted to developing country needs. Equipment, systems and processes easy to understand, operate and maintain. Recycling of treated wastewater possible depending on the type of unit. Assistance in setting up of associated laboratory facilities. Training of local manpower to take over operation and maintenance. Environmental Impact Assessment studies. Carrying out of Environmental Audits / Due Diligence studies. Cleaner Production assignments to reduce pollution at source. Assisting industries with respect to their legal issues, especially related to the National Green Tribunal, with the competency to present facts effectively to the jury members of NGT.
